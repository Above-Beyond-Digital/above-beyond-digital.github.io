7 · Read-through for CIJ 2026

True sale MER ≈ 4.7×. Shopify net ÷ sale-campaign spend was 4.66× (2024) and 4.72× (2025) — consistent and healthy. Moderate headroom to scale Meta; expect MER to compress as spend grows, so scale in steps and watch live.
Size 2026 off 2024 (7-day shape). 2024 = $6,026 net on $1,292 spend over 8 days (~$753 net/day, ~$161 spend/day). A 7-day 2026 at a higher daily spend should grow net sales proportionally while MER holds in the 4× range.
AOV climbing ($104→$120 US) and CA is bundle-led + higher value — lean 2026 creative + email into bundles.
Canada earns on $0 paid ($1.5–3k Shopify net). A light paid test is low-risk upside — resolve Bill 96 French compliance first.
Keep the email pre-sale tease. Both markets ran a "big sale Friday" reveal in 2025. No paid pre-sale, but the email heads-up is part of the FLS playbook.
Email is a major sale driver. On the Shopify sale, email/SMS was the attributed last-touch on 10 of 23 US orders, and in CA attributed revenue exceeded total net sales — email effectively carried the whole (small) CA sale. That was email only; 2026 adds launch + final-hours SMS, so treat email/SMS as a core revenue channel, not an afterthought. CA over-indexes (high-AOV, zero-paid).
