MESSAGING DIRECTION

The angle for this sale: Christmas in July is a real reason to buy now: 15% off everything, and because it stacks on the bundles, savings reach up to 40%. Frame it as the moment to sort the cupboards out, finish the set, and get the clutter under control. The discount is the gift, not a hard sell. The only urgency is the genuine end date (29 July).

Sale prominence: on every offer-bearing asset, "Sale" plus the discount (e.g. "15–40% Off*") is the single loudest element, louder than the "Christmas in July" lockup. On-brand pill or seal, never garish.

Hooks. Cold leads with a pain point or benefit and weaves the sale in. Warm leads with the sale and weaves the pain point/benefits in. Lead on the problem solved (cupboard clutter, the mismatched-container chaos, wasted space) or the benefit (space back, less stress), not a product walkthrough. These are direction, not a script.
