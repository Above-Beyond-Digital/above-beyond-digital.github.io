Feel (brief — the key visual is the real reference): white-dominant (~60%), clean studio product or warm-neutral organised-kitchen lifestyle, 2–3 container colours visible, natural/studio light. Avoid fake snow/tinsel/garish red-green — "Christmas in July" is the reason, not a heavy visual theme.

5. Copy format rules

We supply the actual copy (see copy/). If you set any on-image text:

  • No em dashes. US spelling (organized, color).
  • ALL CAPS is fine for SALE and short callouts (e.g. SALE, 15–40% OFF). Avoid shouty full-sentence caps (HURRY! LIMITED TIME!!).
  • Sentence case for subtext, title case for headlines — both fine.
  • SALE 15–40% Off* is the largest element on every offer asset.

6. Compliance (creative team)

  • Put the asterisk on the offer claim on every offer asset (e.g. 15–40% Off*). The full footnote text does NOT go on the creative — it sits in the ad copy.
  • Keep the wording to "15–40% Off*" or "up to 40%". Do NOT write "40% off the bundles" — the 40% is total savings from stacking the 15% on already-discounted bundles, not 40% off each bundle.
