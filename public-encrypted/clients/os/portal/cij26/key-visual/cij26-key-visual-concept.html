Four-layer hierarchy

  • 1Sale lockup — on a sale asset the big "Sale" + "15-40% Off*" is the single dominant element (as shown), with a short Flat Stacks line ("Goodbye, clutter") as the eyebrow. No banned words, no em dashes, no ALL-CAPS urgency.
  • 2Visual demo — the highest-converting element: show the collapse with food inside or the chaos→organised transformation. Product is always the hero; the demo carries half the message.
  • 3Proof — one specific, provable claim (60% space saved · 4.9 stars from 4,000+ reviews · 5-year warranty) plus the offer.
  • 4CTA — Green soft pill, white Roboto Bold, approved verb-first text ("Shop the Sale" / "Shop Now").

Keep it uncluttered: one hero, lots of white, badge tucked top-right, copy in the footer band, never over the product. Real kitchens and real hands beat studio sterility. Always show the asterisk near the offer.

06 — Art Direction Note

The one rule for CIJ26

Christmas in July is a calm, treat-your-kitchen feeling: the festive nod comes from green-on-white and the stacked-bars mark (a little tree), not gold, red or snow. The product, the collapse and the chaos→organised payoff are always the hero. A brand that sells decluttering must look uncluttered.

Do

  • Show the collapse with food inside, or the chaos→organised transformation
  • Keep it clean and airy: ~60% white, one hero, room to breathe
  • Bright, warm, natural light; real kitchens, real hands
  • Show the colour-coded containers popping against white
  • Lead with a specific proof claim (60% space · 4.9 stars · 5-yr warranty)
  • "Sale" + the discount % are the single most prominent element on every offer asset
  • Asterisk wherever the offer appears (compliance)

Avoid

  • Clutter or busy/patterned backgrounds competing with the product
  • Gold, red, snow, tinsel or any winter cliché
  • ALL-CAPS urgency ("HURRY!", "LAST CHANCE!") — a bargain signal
  • Cold, clinical or dark moody lighting; generic stock photos
  • Product shown without demonstrating what it does
  • Banned words (innovative, multi-functional, solutions, affordable...), health/germ-kill claims, em dashes
