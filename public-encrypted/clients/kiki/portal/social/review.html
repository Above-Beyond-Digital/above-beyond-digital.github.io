How this works

  • Review, rate (stars) and set a status on each post. Approve is the green button; other statuses are under More.
  • Approving one variant of a pair (P1 / P1B) automatically rejects the other. Only the approved one holds the slot.
  • Drag a card by its handle, or drag a calendar thumbnail, to reschedule. The lock icon means a pinned anniversary date that cannot move.
  • Park a post to take it off the schedule for now: the dates after it shift up to fill the gap. Unpark to restore its place.
  • Click any image to view it full size. Click a calendar thumbnail to open the full editor.
  • Search filters the Posts tab and highlights matches on the calendar. Drag reordering is paused while searching or filtering.
  • Edits save to your browser instantly. Backup ▾ exports a JSON copy.

Parked — off the schedule

Each scheduled post shows as its image on its day. Drag a thumbnail to any other day to reschedule it (the date changes, the time stays the same). Click a thumbnail to open the full editor. Posts with a lock icon are pinned to a real-world anniversary and cannot be moved. Smaller faded chips are past posts already published.
