2. Recommended budget — CIJ26

Pre-sale (10–16 Jul, ~$700/day → ~$4,900 total)

  • Engaged-heavy structure (per SOP) — engaged/holdout audiences primary, cold secondary.
  • Run the lead-opt vs purchase-opt test as two separate campaigns; judge on cost-per-acquired-customer.
  • Prerequisite: landing page rebuilt (1.66% → target ~15%+ capture).
  • Target: beat MN10's 464 signups; with a fixed LP + engaged focus, ~700–900+ signups at CPL ~$6–10.

Sale (18–31 Jul, 14 days)

Total-account daily: $3,500–$4,000/day → ~$49k–$56k over 14 days. Below MN10's actual $4,466/day — this is an efficiency-led plan: beat last year on a held/better MER + 14 days vs 11 + deeper discount + better structure, NOT on more spend.
