Campaign in one line: Maria's warm, generous mid-year self-treat moment, 20% OFF*, the 10 Way Necklace™ is the hero in every frame, Christmas in July is just the reason to buy now.

Who: Women 45–65 (35–44 secondary), AU-first.

Brand visual rules

DO AVOID
Warm lifestyle, jewellery worn + styled on skin White / clinical studio backgrounds
Confident happy women 45–65, real mature hands, natural light Stock imagery, anonymous young-model hands
Show "one necklace, ten looks" + the magnetic click Text over the top of the product or faces
Palette: MN Pink #EF6693, Charcoal #222222, pearl/blush warmth Aggressive All Caps urgency (HURRY!)
10 Way Necklace™ with ™ every time, AU English Em-dashes, US spellings, decorative fonts
Max 3 text elements per static; product stays the hero Banned words: addictive, customers, elevate, solutions

Social proof (verified, use as-is, do not round): 4.9★ from 7,500+ reviews; 4,900 Sparkle Sisterhood members.

Tracking: every asset name carries the CIJ26 code.

Specs & safe zones (read once, applies to everything)

  • Statics: 4:5 (1080×1350). Keep all core content + text inside the centre 1:1 (1080×1080) safe zone so each crops cleanly to a 1:1 carousel frame.
  • Videos + reels: 9:16 (1080×1920). Keep core content + text inside the 4:5 safe zone so they read on feed and in-stream too.
  • No separate story/reel statics — one 4:5 master static, cropped as needed.
