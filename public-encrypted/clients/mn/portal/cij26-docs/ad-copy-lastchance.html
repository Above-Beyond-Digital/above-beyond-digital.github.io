CIJ26 — Meta Ad Copy: LAST-CHANCE PHASE (29–31 Jul)

What this is: a creative + copy refresh for the final three days of Christmas in July. It does NOT spin up new campaigns or ad sets. These ads drop into the EXISTING sale ad sets (the warm/PPS ad set and the cold ASC), swapping last-chance copy onto the best-performing main-sale creatives (per Master Brief §4.2). Tone: warmth first, genuine final-days urgency only as the close. The sale really does end Friday 31 July, 11:59pm AEST, so urgency is honest. Keep "Last chance" and "final hours" to the genuine final-days framing here, never false scarcity. Voice: warm Maria best-friend. AU English (jewellery, colour, organise, travelling, favourite). NO em-dashes. 10 Way Necklace™ with ™ on EVERY instance. Max 1 emoji per primary text. Banned: addictive, customers (use Ladies / Sisters / gorgeous women), elevate, solutions, curated, timeless elegance, leverage, optimize, empower, "world's most versatile necklace", price-shaming, anti-ageing, competitor comparisons. The offer is always written 20% off* with the exclusion footnote nearby. NEVER "20% off sitewide" or "20% off everything". The * footnote (verbatim): Excludes Beginner Bundles, Gift Cards and Flash Sale items. Discount mechanics: automatic discount at checkout, NO code (public window). No VIP code in last-chance ads. Tracking code: CIJ26 in every ad name (these reuse the existing sale ad sets, so adset/campaign names already carry it). Verified social proof (use as-is, no rounding): 4.9★ from 7,500+ reviews · 4,900 Sparkle Sisterhood members. Document 2 = In-sale non-buyers (drops into the existing cold/ASC + warm ad sets, targeting people who engaged or visited during the sale but have not purchased).
