Two sale campaigns. Warm + PPS sit in one campaign as two ad sets (ABO).

Sale creative in evergreen cold hurt every time (MN10 4.17× vs dedicated 7.28×) — keep it out.

7. Pacing (SOP §4 / Master Brief §4.3)

  1. Hands-off the first ~3 days (19–21 Jul) — let ad sets exit learning. Don't ruin learnings. (Can turn off clearly non-performing creatives in the first few days — Master Brief §4.3.)
  2. After that, reallocate in ≤20% steps — bigger jumps re-trigger learning even on a stable ad set.
  3. Move budget to where ROAS holds with frequency in check. Don't starve TOF — it feeds the rest.
  4. PPS ROAS is inflated by the email/SMS halo — don't over-shift to PPS; keep the cap.
  5. Protect cold acquisition budget — don't let warm cannibalise it (the reason cold is its own campaign).
  6. Budget is reallocatable between the sale campaigns and evergreen during the sale based on performance (SOP §4).
