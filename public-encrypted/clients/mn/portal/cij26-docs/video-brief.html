Christmas in July 2026 — Your Video Scripts

Hi Maria. Three short videos to record yourself, to camera, on your phone. Talk to the lens like you are ringing a Sparkle Sister to share good news. Warm, and personable. You do not need to read these word for word, say them in your own words.

The lengths below are a guide, not a limit — a little over or under is completely fine. Say what feels natural.

Video 1 — Pre-sale

Purpose: Tell the Sisters the sale is coming, get them onto the VIP SMS list for early access. The pull is the 20% off + getting in before everyone else, because the popular pieces sell out fast. Length guide: around 45–60 seconds.

Hook (first 3 seconds): "Ladies, one of our biggest sales of the year is coming."

Full script: "Ladies, Maria here, and one of our biggest sales of the year is coming. It is our Christmas in July sale, 20% off, and our VIP list gets in a full day early, before everyone else.

(hold up a popular 10 Way Necklace™ piece and do a quick styling change) If you have not seen it before, this is our 10 Way Necklace™. One necklace, and look, ten different ways to wear it. The magnet just clicks together, on and off in a second.

(hold up one or two more popular pieces — a bright crystal, the Cream Pearl) These are some of the pieces the Sisters love most, and here is the thing, lovely, the popular colours sell out so fast every year. I would hate for you to miss your favourite.

So here is how you get in first. Sign up to our SMS list, and you will get early access on the 18th of July, before anyone else, so you get first pick. Just tap the button below to sign up. Join me there, gorgeous."

What to wear / show:

  • Wear something simple and warm-toned so each piece reads clearly against you.
  • Have 2–3 popular pieces ready to lift one at a time. Hold each up close and turn it in the light so the sparkle and pearl shine.
  • Do a quick "ten ways" change so newcomers see how it works. Show-and-tell with a friend, not a pitch.

Video 2 — Main sale (the most important one)

Purpose: Open the sale to everyone and drive sales. 20% off everything, no code needed. This is where most of the budget goes, so the hook has to be strong. Length guide: around 60 seconds.

Hook (first 3 seconds): "Ladies, it is 20% off right now for Christmas in July."

Full script: "Ladies, Maria here, and it is 20% off for our Christmas in July sale, with no code to remember, your discount is applied for you at the checkout.

(hold up the 10 Way Necklace™ and run through a few quick changes) And this is what I want you to treat yourself to. Our 10 Way Necklace™ gives you ten looks from a single necklace. One piece, styled ten different ways, on and off in a second with the magnet.

(show a couple of the most popular colours, then a connector or pendant) Here are some of our most-loved colours, and these little connectors and pendants let you make it your own.

Come and have a look while your favourite is still here, because the popular colours do sell out fast. Head to the website right now, the sale is on until Friday the 31st of July. Do not miss out, gorgeous. See you there."

What to wear / show:

  • Wear festive-warm but tasteful, and layer your favourite 10 Way Necklace™.
  • Demonstrate the product: a few quick "ten ways" changes, the magnetic click, popular colours, and an accessory or two.
  • Warm and inviting, but make the 20% off and the end date land clearly.

Film this one TWICE:

  • Once upright (vertical) like the others, for social.
  • Once on its side (landscape) for YouTube. Only this video needs the landscape version.

Video 3 — Last chance

Purpose: A warm, genuine final reminder. The sale ends Friday at midnight. Length guide: under 30 seconds.

Hook (first 3 seconds): "Ladies, this is your last chance."

Full script: "Ladies, Maria here, and this is your last chance to get 20% Off. Our Christmas in July sale ends this Friday at midnight.

(hold up a hero piece, give the magnet a quick click — not a full demo) If there is a piece you have been thinking about, now is the moment. (show one or two of the popular pieces) Our most-loved colours sell out fast, so pop yours in the basket before Friday. No code needed, it auto-applies at checkout.

I would hate for you to miss out, lovely. See you before midnight, gorgeous."

What to wear / show:

  • Same warm, real styling, with a 10 Way Necklace™ layered on.
  • Quickly show a hero piece and the magnetic click (a quick show, not a full demonstration), plus a couple of popular items.
  • Reiterate the deadline. Warm and caring, a friend reminding you, not a countdown clock.

Filming tips

  • Natural light is your friend. Film near a window, facing the light, morning or late afternoon. Avoid harsh overhead lights.
  • Look down the lens, not at yourself on the screen. It makes the Sisters feel like you are talking straight to them.
  • Hold pieces up close and turn them slowly so the sparkle, pearl and colour catch the light.
  • Do 2–3 takes of each and pick your favourite. The most natural one is usually best, not the most polished.
  • Film vertical (portrait) on your phone, keep the background simple, and speak a touch slower than feels natural. (Exception: also film the main-sale video #2 in landscape, on its side, for YouTube.)
