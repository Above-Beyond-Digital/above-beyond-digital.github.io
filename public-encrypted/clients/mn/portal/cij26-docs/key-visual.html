06 — Art Direction Note

The one rule for CIJ26

Christmas in July is a warm, celebratory treat-yourself feeling — festive joy carried by tasteful gold and a tiny sprig, on MN's blush and pink. No snow, no winter, no red, no knits or fireplaces. The 10 Way Necklace™ always stays the hero.

Do

  • Warm, sun-drenched blush and pink backgrounds
  • Gold, and green very sparingly, as small accents (no red)
  • Real women 45-65, jewellery on skin, natural light
  • Tasteful round Sale 20% Off* seal, never a starburst
  • "Sale" + the discount % are the single most prominent element on every asset — larger and louder than the campaign name and the headline
  • 10 Way Necklace™ with ™ on every instance

Avoid

  • Snow, frost, winter or cosy-knit / fireplace clichés
  • Garish festive colour that drowns the pink
  • Aggressive sale starbursts, ALL-CAPS urgency
  • Busy, dark or patterned backgrounds
  • Em-dashes in any on-asset copy
